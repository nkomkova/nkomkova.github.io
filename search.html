Expanding peaceful exploration of space for the benefit of humanity

Welcome to Asgardia!

For the first time in history a new nation state has been created - not on Earth but in the heavens above. Asgardia is the prototype of a free and unrestricted society which holds knowledge, intelligence and science at its core along with the recognition of the ultimate value of each human life. You can join like-minded people on this new exciting step in fostering an extended future for humankind.

Igor Ashurbeiyli

The founding father of Asgardia

In only 40 hours since I have announced the birth of the new space nation Asgardia one hundred thousand people from more than two hundred countries on Earth have applied to join.

And 4 days later we are already more than 300 000 people.

I would like to welcome and thank you personally.

Now we are continuing the registration aiming to bring together one million people. Soon we will become a member of the UN.

At this moment the top three countries of the world where Asgardians reside are China, United States of America and Turkey. Among more than 240 countries on Earth Asgardia is now number 188 in terms of the population. It is between Samoa and Barbados.

Thanks again for your support. Together we are Asgardians.

Opening speech by Dr. Igor Ashurbeyli, the founding father of Asgardia, at the press-conference in Paris on October 12, 2016.
